How to export reports from Google Ads
1
Open Google Ads and sign in
Go to ads.google.com and select your account.
2
Set your date range
Use the date picker (top right) — "Last 30 days" is recommended. Keep it consistent across all reports.
3
Export the Campaigns report
Left sidebar → Campaigns → click the download icon (↓) → CSVcampaigns.csv
4
Export the Keywords report
Left sidebar → Keywords → Keyword → download → CSVkeywords.csv
5
Export the Search Terms report
Left sidebar → Keywords → Search terms → download → CSVsearch_terms.csv
6
Export the Ads report
Left sidebar → Ads & assets → Ads → download → CSVads.csv
7
Upload all files and analyze
Switch to the "Upload reports" tab, drop all CSVs, enter your API key, and click Analyze.
